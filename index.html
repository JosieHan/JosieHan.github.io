Get Cooking Accurately With the Meld Knob + Clip

1 April, 2020

Many people enjoy making a home cooked meal. Unfortunately, the joys of cooking can quickly turn to frustration. Your family or a phone call distracts you and now the food’s burnt. You find a recipe that looks promising but vague directions or differences in cookware ruin your efforts.

Lazy cooks or those that value accuracy might find a possible solution to their frustrations in the Meld Knob + Clip.

The Meld Knob is an automated device that can attach to your existing cooktop and cookware to adjust your burner’s cooking temperature. The result, as Meld claims, is a precisely controlled cooking temperature that is accurate to within less than a degree. There are hundreds of chef-tested recipes available in the mobile App which can help to automatically control the burner. Besides, users can make their own recipe or manually control the knob if they want.

Now the company has taken to Kickstarter to launch its first product. The Meld Knob easily surpassed its campaign goal and have raised more than $150,000.
